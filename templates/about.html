About Phish Guard Pro

Protecting Dhaka International University from Cyber Threats

Phish Guard Pro is an innovative project developed by Dhaka International University to combat phishing attacks and enhance cybersecurity awareness among students, faculty, and staff. Our mission is to create a secure digital environment by leveraging cutting-edge technology and comprehensive training programs.
